Our Story

Building a legacy of excellence in pool care since 2010

At Blue Pool Clean, we provide three customized swimming pool and spa cleaning and maintenance options designed for residential customers. Our experienced, uniformed pool technicians ensure professional service every time.

Premium Weekly Swimming Pool and Spa Service

Enjoy comprehensive pool and spa care with our weekly service. Unlike "Every Other Week" plans offered by others, we ensure your pool receives full attention every single week, keeping it ready for any occasion—be it a party or unexpected weather.

Service Includes:

  • ✔ Chemical testing and adjustments
  • ✔ Pool surface skimming
  • ✔ Thorough vacuuming
  • ✔ Emptying pool and pump baskets
  • ✔ Water level checks
  • ✔ Wall brushing
  • ✔ Tile cleaning
  • ✔ Cartridge filter cleaning or backwashing
  • ✔ Equipment inspection
