Privacy Policy

At Crystal Pool Services San Francisco, your privacy isn’t just a checkbox—it’s a promise. This policy dives deep into how we gather, use, and safeguard your personal information. By navigating our website, you’re giving a nod to this Privacy Policy.

What We Collect

We don’t snoop where we shouldn’t. The only personal info we gather is what you willingly hand over, such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • IP address

This info fuels our ability to respond to your inquiries, deliver top-notch services, and fine-tune your user experience. Sensitive data? Collected only with your clear, unmistakable consent.

How We Use Your Information

Your data isn’t just sitting idle. Here’s what we do with it:

  • Crafting responses to your questions or service requests.
  • Enhancing our website’s usability, because clunky sites are so 2005.
  • Sending updates, tips, or exclusive promos (but only if you say “yes”).

Sharing your info? Never, unless the law forces our hand. Selling or renting your data? Absolutely not.

Cookies & Analytics

Cookies—tiny, magical files that make your browsing smoother. Think of them as digital butlers remembering your preferences and keeping our site running at its best. Disable them through your browser if you must, but expect a few hiccups.

We also harness the power of Google Analytics to study website traffic and user habits. Don’t worry, this data is anonymized, and you can always opt out using Google’s tools if it feels too Big Brother for your taste.

How We Guard Your Data

Your information deserves VIP treatment. That’s why we’ve equipped our site with standard security measures to fend off breaches. But hey, even Fort Knox has its limits.

If a breach sneaks through? You’ll be the first to know, and we’ll act swiftly to minimize any fallout.

How Long We Keep Your Info

We’re not hoarders. Personal data sticks around only as long as it’s needed:

  • Contact form submissions? Gone once your query’s resolved.
  • Security logs? Purged after 60 days.

Want your info erased or updated? Just ask.

Third-Party Content

Embedded videos? External links? Those shiny extras might come with their own data-collection quirks. Check out their privacy policies for the full scoop.

Your Rights, Your Call

You have the power to:

  • Request your data.
  • Fix any incorrect details.
  • Ask us to delete your info.

But keep in mind, some data might need to stick around to meet legal obligations.

Policy Updates

Privacy practices evolve. When we tweak this policy, you’ll always find the freshest version right here.
